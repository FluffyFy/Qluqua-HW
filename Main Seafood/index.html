Exciting News: Sea Harvest Reopens in Belconnen!

We are thrilled to announce the reopening of our Belconnen store on December 13th!

After a brief hiatus, we are back and better than ever, ready to serve the community with the finest seafood selections at 15 Market Street, Belconnen. Our store has undergone a revitalization to enhance your shopping experience, featuring a modern layout and a vibrant atmosphere.

From succulent prawns to delectable salmon, our premium seafood is sourced directly from trusted suppliers to ensure top quality.

Whether you are a seafood enthusiast or a casual shopper, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are dedicated to providing personalized service to meet all your culinary needs. Join us at our Belconnen location as we dive back into delivering a sea of flavors to your table!

We can't wait to welcome you back!
